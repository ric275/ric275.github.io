A growing collection of old, fruity computers.

Here is a complete list of what I consider to be the major products that make up my collection of Apple devices. I've organised it by year so one can get a general idea of what stage Apple was at in said year. Any new items will be added to the list as and when. I also plan on adding photos of these devices over time. I'm always looking to grow my collection so if you have any devices in need of a home, let me know!

If you're curious, my Hackett Number is 21.
